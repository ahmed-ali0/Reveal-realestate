About Revel

Finding the right home for you has always been a time-consuming and stressful process, but that has all changed with Revel. We have helped more than 100,000 families find their dream home and meet their needs in the shortest possible time. As a real estate technology company, we are keen to provide many diverse services to our clients, which include real estate brokerage between our clients and real estate development companies, while providing a comparison between different units in terms of price, area, number of rooms and many other factors, in addition to the possibility of offering your residential unit for sale through us. At Revel, we did not stop at this point only, but we worked on launching the “Live Now, Pay Later” service, which enables you to obtain the unit of your dreams and move into it immediately, with installments for up to 10 years. This came thanks to a partnership contract between us and “Contact”, one of the companies of the Contact Financial Holding Group, worth one billion Egyptian pounds, with the aim of financing our clients to purchase the real estate units they want. We currently have over 200 employees working passionately across various departments to deliver an exceptional experience to our customers, using the latest methods and advanced technological techniques. Despite all the successes we have achieved in a short period of time, we see that we are still at the beginning of the road, as we at Revel aim to benefit as much as possible from the digital transformation, revolutionize the world of real estate, and help each of our clients make their purchasing decision easily and obtain their dream home.
